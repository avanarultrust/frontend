At Avanarul Trust, our mission is simple yet profound: Bringing Change Where It Matters Most. Guided by grace and driven by a strong sense of purpose, we work tirelessly to uplift communities. Whether it is through the preservation and renovation of ancient Hindu temples, launching accessible medical awareness programs, or standing strong behind the youth with educational support, our focus is always set on making a meaningful impact.

Our Founder's Story

Mr. S. Raja Pandian

"Bringing Change Where It Matters Most."

Mr. S. Raja Pandian, the esteemed founder of Avanarul Trust and Avanarul Properties, is a man whose life story is defined by extraordinary resilience and a deep-rooted commitment to service.

Forced to discontinue his studies at the age of eight due to family circumstances, he began working at a very young age to support his parents. While his formal schooling was cut short, his thirst for knowledge never ceased; he is a self-taught intellectual who has gained a vast education through reading extensively and maintaining a dedicated pursuit of learning.

Despite early hardships, he maintained a profoundly devotional and service-minded outlook, establishing a lifelong tradition of providing food for people on every Pradhosham and donating food and sweets to charities during festivals and special occasions.

His ability to inspire others led him to involve his friends in various social works, a spirit of leadership that also fueled his entrepreneurial success as the founder of the Paatikadai Restaurant brand, which has successfully grown into a business with multiple franchises.

Beyond his business ventures, Mr. Raja Pandian is a multifaceted public figure, serving as a Chief Reporter for a Tamil monthly magazine and as a spokesperson for a Central Government-authorized Hindu party. His spiritual dedication remains a cornerstone of his life, evidenced by his extensive work in temple renovations and ongoing devotional services.
